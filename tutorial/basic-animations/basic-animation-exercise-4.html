Exercise 4 - Moving in rectangle (challenge)

Now you know how to let the animation move left, right, top and down as well as diagonally. How about you try to let the move in a rectangle clockwise. So this is what you need to achieve:

  1. Move right 400px

  2. Move down 200px

  3. Move left 400px

  4. Then move back up 200px

  5. Each move will take 2 seconds to run.

      6. You may have to change the timing to {startDelay: 2, duration: 2} from '2' at certain places. Note that startDelay starts counting from the start of the animation.


      Show Solution
      new Animation(document.querySelector("#a"), {left: "400px"}, 2);
      new Animation(document.querySelector("#a"), {top: "200px"}, {startDelay: 2, duration: 2});
      new Animation(document.querySelector("#a"), {left: "0px"}, {startDelay: 4, duration: 2});
      new Animation(document.querySelector("#a"), {top: "0px"}, {startDelay: 6, duration: 2});
